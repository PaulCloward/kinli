What is the Bio Basin?
The Bio Basin is a light weight basin designed to improve fluid collection in a variety of clinical settings. It has been proven effective for irrigation of various lacerations, extremities, eyes, ear canals and other procedures. This product is patented and designed by hospital personnel with over 25 years of patient care experience.
Hear it from the Clinicians
“Whether cleansing hand, foot, or head wounds, the Bio basin is the practical basin to use while irrigating, without creating a mess. No balancing the extremity on an emesis basin or folding pads to direct fluid into the trash can. While repairing the wound, the procedure can continue without redressing the surgical field. At the conclusion of the procedure, the basin is easily removed to allow disposal of the fluid. I use it every night I am in the Emergency Department, or for referrals taken care of in the office.”
- Eric Vanderhooft M.D.
